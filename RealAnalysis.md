### Cauchy Schwartz Inequality:
$$
\left(\sum^n_{i=1}a_ib_i \right)^2  \leq \left(\sum^n_{i=1}a_i^2\right)\left(\sum^n_{i=1}b_i^2\right)
$$
> Equality Exist only when $b_i = \lambda a_i \text{  }(\forall  a_i,b_i)$
